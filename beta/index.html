OUR PRODUCTS & SOLUTIONS

Rhine Energy has full fledged manufacturing setup at IMT Manesar, Haryana and has installed capacity to manufacture more than 300 compact substations per annum and customized E-House solutions. We have state of the art facilty for sheet steel fabrication with japanese CNC machines and automated vertical pre treatment and robotic based powder painting plant with our associates in Manesar.
Compact Substation

Compact Substation upto 36kV

Rhine Energy is a licensed technology partner of Siemens to manufacture Compact Sub-stations (CSS type 8FB20). Under this licence partnership Rhine Energy is to integrate Siemens Ring Main Unit (RMU) of rating 12kV/24kV, 630A, 21kA in 8FB20 compact substation. The compact substatio in fully type tested as per lastest IEC 62271-202 and manufacured under high quality standards.

E-House

E-House

We offer complete one stop solution to execute the project of E-House from concept to commissioning. The electrical house(E-House) is latest market trend in electrical powerdistribution. E-House is ready to use solution andconsist of one or several modules equipped withhigh-quality components, including mediumvoltage air insulated switchgear (AIS) or gas–insulated switchgear (GIS), low voltage switchboards,power transformers, protection, monitoring andcontrol systems, Power management systems(PMS) and building management systems.

MV Switchgear

MV Switchgear/Ring Main Units

Rhine Energy serves the Indian market with a wide range of Medium voltage Power Distribution Systems. Our products from the MV Distribution product family are designed keeping in mind the fast growing power needs of Indian cities, combined with reduced urban space availability. We integrate and supply Siemens make IPAN switchgear upto 33kV. We also integrate and supply Ring Main Unit Solutions.
